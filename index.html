Zhenkai Zhang, Ph.D.
Assistant Professor
Department of Computer Science
Texas Tech University



Zhenkai Zhang received his Ph.D. degree from Vanderbilt University, under the supervision of Prof. Xenofon Koutsoukos. He earned his M.S. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and B.S. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology. Prior to joining Texas Tech, he was a research scientist at Institute for Software Integrated Systems and conducted research on cyber-physical/real-time/embedded systems. Currently, his research interests are in computer systems security, especially hardware security.
